# ARKHA — Product Document

## What is ARKHA?

ARKHA is an AI-powered resume intelligence platform that helps job seekers analyze, optimize, and tailor their applications to specific job listings—while also helping them discover relevant jobs.

At its core, ARKHA answers one painful question:

> **“Why am I not getting hired?”**

Instead of guessing, it replaces uncertainty with data, automation, and targeted improvement.

---

## Core Value Proposition

ARKHA helps users:

- Match better  
- Apply smarter  
- Get hired faster  

Instead of blindly sending resumes, using generic templates, or guessing what recruiters want, ARKHA provides a complete system that includes match scoring, optimized resumes, tailored cover letters, and job discovery—all in one workflow.

---

## What ARKHA Offers

### Resume Match Score

ARKHA compares a user’s resume against a job description and outputs a percentage-based match score while highlighting gaps.

---

### AI Resume Optimization

The platform rewrites resumes for specific roles by aligning them with relevant keywords, required skills, and role-specific phrasing.

---

### Harvard-Style Resume Export

ARKHA generates clean, professional, ATS-friendly resumes that are ready to send as PDFs.

---

### Cover Letter Generator

Cover letters are automatically generated using both the resume and job description.

---

### Job Discovery (OnlineJobs.ph Integration)

ARKHA pulls real job listings including full-time, part-time, and gig opportunities.

---

### Analyze Jobs Directly

Users can analyze job listings instantly without copy-pasting content elsewhere.

## Key Problems ARKHA Solves

- Lack of interview responses → Match scoring + gap analysis  
- Generic resumes → AI optimization  
- Time-consuming applications → Automation  
- Weak formatting → Structured export  
- Writing cover letters → Auto-generation  
- Scattered job search → Built-in job discovery

## Product Flow

1. Upload resume  
2. Select or find a job  
3. Get match score + gaps  
4. Optimize resume  
5. Generate cover letter  
6. Download and apply  
7. Repeat  

---

## UX Insight

Everything happens in one place.

Avoid:
- External tools  
- Complex flows  
- Too many steps  

---

## Risks

- Becoming just another resume tool  
- Weak differentiation from AI tools  
- Overpromising hiring outcomes  

Use:
- “Increase your chances” instead of guarantees
